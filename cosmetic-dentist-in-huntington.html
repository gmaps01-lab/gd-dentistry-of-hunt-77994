How long does it take to get veneers or other cosmetic dental work done?
Most cosmetic procedures like veneers require two to three visits over a span of two to three weeks. Teeth whitening can often be completed in a single one-hour visit. We try to accommodate busy schedules and will provide a personalized timeline during your initial visit to our Huntington, NY location.
