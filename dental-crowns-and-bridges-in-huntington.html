Frequently Asked Questions About Dental Crowns and Bridges

How much do dental crowns and bridges typically cost at GD Dentistry Of Huntington?
The cost of dental crowns at GD Dentistry Of Huntington usually ranges from $1,200 to $1,500 per crown, depending on the materials used and the complexity of your case. Dental bridges, which replace one or more missing teeth, generally range from $2,800 to $4,000. We always provide a detailed estimate after your consultation, and our staff can help you navigate insurance coverage and available payment plans.
How long does it take to get a crown or bridge at your Huntington office?
The process for crowns or bridges typically takes two visits to our Huntington office. During the first visit, we prepare your tooth, take digital impressions, and fit you with a temporary crown or bridge. The final restoration is custom-made by a local dental lab, which usually takes about 2 weeks. At your second visit, we secure the permanent crown or bridge and ensure the fit and appearance meet your expectations.
Are your dentists licensed and experienced in performing crowns and bridges?
Yes, all of our dentists at GD Dentistry Of Huntington are fully licensed in New York State and have extensive experience in restorative dentistry, including crowns and bridges. Dr. Green and Dr. Davis attend ongoing training to stay current with the latest techniques and materials, ensuring you receive the highest standard of care.
What is the process like for getting a dental crown or bridge?
After a thorough exam and consultation, we will prepare the affected tooth or teeth by gently reshaping them. We use digital scanning technology to take precise impressions, which are then sent to a local lab in Huntington for custom fabrication. You’ll wear a temporary crown or bridge for about two weeks, allowing us to protect your tooth while your permanent restoration is made. During your final visit, we’ll securely bond your new crown or bridge and make any necessary adjustments for comfort and appearance.
Do you offer any guarantees or warranties on crowns and bridges?
At GD Dentistry Of Huntington, we stand behind the quality of our work. We offer a 5-year limited warranty on our crowns and bridges, provided you maintain regular check-ups and cleanings at our office. If you experience any issues related to the fit or function of your restoration during this period, we’ll address them at no additional charge.
