Most root canal procedures at our Huntington office are completed in one to two appointments. Each visit usually lasts between 60 and 90 minutes. However, if your tooth has complex roots or infection, a second visit may be needed to ensure complete treatment and healing.

What is the process for getting a root canal at your Huntington office?

Afterward, we typically recommend placing a crown to protect the tooth, which can be scheduled here in our Huntington office.
