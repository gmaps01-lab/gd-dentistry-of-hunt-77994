What is the process for getting a dental crown at your office?
Getting a dental crown at GD Dentistry Of Huntington usually involves two visits. During the first visit, we’ll examine the tooth, take digital impressions, and prepare the area. You’ll leave with a temporary crown. Our local partner lab in Suffolk County crafts your custom crown, which typically takes about 10-14 days. Once it’s ready, you’ll return for a quick, comfortable fitting and permanent placement.
