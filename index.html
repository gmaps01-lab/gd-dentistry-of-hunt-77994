Frequently Asked Questions About Implant Dentist

How much do dental implants typically cost at GD Dentistry Of Huntington, and are payment plans available?
At GD Dentistry Of Huntington, the cost of a single dental implant generally ranges from $3,500 to $4,500, depending on your specific needs and whether bone grafting or additional procedures are required. We understand that implants are an investment, so we offer flexible payment plans and can assist you with financing options. Our team is happy to provide a detailed estimate after your initial consultation and will discuss all available payment solutions to help make your treatment affordable.
How long does the dental implant process take from start to finish?
The dental implant process at our Huntington office typically takes 3 to 6 months from start to finish. This includes the initial consultation, any necessary preparatory procedures (such as tooth extraction or bone grafting), implant placement, healing time for the implant to fuse with the bone (osseointegration), and finally, placement of the crown. Timelines can vary based on your individual treatment plan, and we’ll provide a personalized timeline after your consultation.
Are your dentists licensed and experienced specifically in implant dentistry?
Yes, all dentists performing implant procedures at GD Dentistry Of Huntington are fully licensed in New York State and have advanced training in implant dentistry. Our lead implant dentist has over 15 years of experience placing and restoring implants and regularly participates in continuing education to stay up-to-date with the latest techniques and technologies.
What is the process for getting a dental implant at your Huntington office?
Your journey begins with a comprehensive consultation, where we perform a 3D scan and oral examination to assess your suitability for implants. If you’re a candidate, we’ll schedule the implant placement, which involves placing a titanium post into your jawbone under local anesthesia. After a healing period of several months, during which the implant integrates with your bone, you’ll return for the placement of a custom crown. Throughout the process, our team will guide you step by step and ensure your comfort and understanding.
Do you offer any guarantees or warranties on dental implants?
We stand behind our work at GD Dentistry Of Huntington. All implants placed at our office come with a 5-year warranty covering the implant and restoration, provided you attend regular check-ups and follow our care instructions. If you experience any issues, our team is committed to addressing them promptly and ensuring your long-term satisfaction with your dental implant.
