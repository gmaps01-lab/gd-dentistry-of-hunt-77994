Frequently Asked Questions About Dental Implants

How much do dental implants typically cost at GD Dentistry Of Huntington, and do you offer financing options?
The cost of a single dental implant at GD Dentistry Of Huntington generally ranges from $3,000 to $5,000, depending on your specific needs, including any necessary bone grafts or extractions. We understand that implants are a significant investment, so we offer flexible financing plans and can help you navigate insurance coverage. During your initial consultation, we'll provide a personalized cost estimate and discuss payment options.
What is the typical timeline for getting a dental implant at your Huntington office?
The dental implant process at our Huntington location usually takes several months from start to finish. After your initial consultation, if you're a good candidate, the implant placement surgery is scheduled. It takes about 3-6 months for the implant to fuse with your jawbone (a process called osseointegration). Once healed, we'll place an abutment and custom crown. We’ll provide a detailed timeline during your consultation, as each patient’s needs can vary.
Are your dentists licensed and experienced in performing dental implant procedures?
Absolutely. All dentists at GD Dentistry Of Huntington are licensed in New York State and have advanced training and years of experience in dental implantology. Our team regularly attends continuing education to stay at the forefront of implant techniques and technology, ensuring you receive safe, high-quality care.
What does the dental implant process look like at GD Dentistry Of Huntington?
Our process begins with a comprehensive consultation and 3D imaging to assess your jawbone and oral health. If you’re a candidate, we’ll plan your treatment using state-of-the-art digital tools. The implant is surgically placed in our Huntington office under local anesthesia or sedation. After healing, we create a custom crown that matches your natural teeth. The entire process is managed in-house, so you’ll see the same team at every step.
Do you offer any guarantees or warranties on dental implants?
Yes, at GD Dentistry Of Huntington, we stand behind our work. We offer a limited warranty on dental implants, covering the implant and restoration for up to five years, provided you maintain regular check-ups and proper oral hygiene. We’ll discuss all warranty details and patient responsibilities during your treatment planning.
